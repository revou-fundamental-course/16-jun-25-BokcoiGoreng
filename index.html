About Us

Gacor Garage is where classic vibes meet modern passion. We are a bunch of car lovers who breathe life back into legendary rides like the BMW E36 M3, Toyota AE86 Trueno, Lancer Evo, and other iconic machines from the golden era of automotive culture. Whether it's restoring, modifying, or simply preserving these vintage beasts, we treat every project like a personal obsession because for us, it's more than just business; it's a way of life.

At Gacor Garage, we believe every old-school car has a story worth telling and we're here to make sure it roars again. Our garage is a space for enthusiasts, dreamers, and anyone who appreciates the soul of real driving. Come by, talk cars, share stories, and maybe even start your next build with us. This is more than a garage. This is home for vintage car culture.

Vision & Mission

Vision

We dream of a world where vintage cars never die but still tearing up streets and tracks with pride. Gacor Garage exists to revive and preserve these timeless machines, keeping their spirit alive for the next generation of gearheads to enjoy and celebrate.

Mission

Our mission is to bring forgotten classics back to life by offering top-tier restoration, access to hard-to-find spare parts, and the best accessories tailored for vintage builds. We're here to support fellow enthusiasts in building, maintaining, and loving these iconic rides — one project, one part, one story at a time.
